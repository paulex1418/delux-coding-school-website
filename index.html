FAQ

What is Delux Coding School?

Delux coding school is an institution dedicated to providing high-quality coding and technology education. We offer a variety of programming tracks designed to prepare students for successful careers in tech industry.

Who can enroll in Delux Coding School?

We welcome individuals of all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced coder looking to upskill, students or working professionals, our programs cater to a wide range of students.

What programming languages do you teach?

Our curriculum covers a broad spectrum of programming languages and technologies relevant to the industry. Specific languages and tools vary by track, and our instructors ensure you gain proficiency in the latest industry-standard technologies.

How long are the programs, and what is the schedule like?

Program durations vary depending on the track you choose, ranging from a few weeks to several months. We offer flexible schedulling options, including full-time, part-time, physical and online classes to accommodate your needs.

What are the Pre-requisites for enrolling in a Program?

Pre-requisites vary by track. Some programs require no prior coding experience, While others may have specific technical pre-requisites. Please refer to our brochure or website for detailed information on each track's requirements.

Can i take classes online, or are they only on-site?

You have the option to take classes either on-site at our facility or online. We offer remote learning opportunities with the same high-quality instruction as our on-site programs, providing you with flexibility and convenience.
